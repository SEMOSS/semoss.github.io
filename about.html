Our Mission

SEMOSS is an innovative and data-driven application that allows users to explore and uncover connections among existing data from multiple repositories in an effort to help make more informed decisions.

Why we made it

SEMOSS was built to consolidate data and system information to a central location. The goal was to assist program managers and leaders with planning and migration efforts.

SEMOSS identifies systems that support specific capabilities, data and system functions, to find redundancies to reduce portfolio duplication, and in turn supports development of plans and estimates for modernizing systems.

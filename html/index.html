How it works

Engage Data

Upload your own data or explore our existing databases. With elastic data integration you can extract conclusions from large datasets.

Uncover Connections

Use our predefined algorithms or create your own to use accross connected databases.

Discover and Share Insights

Choose from a rich library of interactive visualizations to better understand your data and guide your decisions.

Easy to use interface

Easily navigate between datasets, interactive visualizations, and customizable features. No need for manuals or intense training courses, SEMOSS lets you jump into your data right away with a straightforward interface.

Access to our databases

Learn even more from your data by adding extra contextual data from our preexisting databases. Similarly, you have the ability to set your share settings so that others can use parts of your data.

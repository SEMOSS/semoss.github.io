Application Healthgrids

Application healthgrids allow users to visualize and compare the health of their different application based on business value, technical maturity, and the financial coordinate.

    Business value is measured based on Dynamic Business Capability Mapping (DBCM). DBCM is based on Theory of Constraints normalized to eliminate bias
    Technical maturity of a legacy program, plan, and / or initiative (PPI) reflects its level of system availability, maintainability, vulnerability, flexibility, etc.
    Financial coordinate is based on the sustainment budget of an application

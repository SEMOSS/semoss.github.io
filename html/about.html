Meet our team.

Our Mission

SEMOSS is an innovative and data-driven application that allows users to explore and uncover connections among existing data from multiple repositories in an effort to help make more informed decisions.

The tool displays data from one or more databases in an interactive format that users can customize, overlay, and extend based on their individual needs. This can help establish connections between the pieces of data that healthcare providers need and those system(s) that provide it.

Why we made it

SEMOSS was built to conslidate data and system information to a central location. The goal was to assist program managers and leaders with planning and migration efforts. SEMOSS does this by supporting development of plans and estimates for modernizing systems. SEMOSS was able to identify systems that support specific capabilities, reduce portfolio duplication by finding redundancies in data and system functions, support alignment with schedules, and help identify interim architectures based on functional needs.

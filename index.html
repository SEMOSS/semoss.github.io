Open Analytics Environment

Seamoss is an innovative and data driven application that allows users to explore and uncover connections among existing data from multiple repositories.

Benefits

Consolidate

Consolidate all of your information into a central location

Identify

Identify ripple effects associated with interface changes

Plan

Assist with planning and migration efforts. Support development of plans for modernizing systems based on Service Oriented Architecture principles

Explore

Explore data and uncover previously unknown relationships

Leadership

    Provide a “Big Picture” view of the Enterprise. Illustrate the benefits of net-centric environment based on SOA principles, and create system lists by location.

Technical

Highlight system and interface dependencies, and llustrate which interfaces can be replaced by services.

Financial

Provide a system’s total budget, identify transition costs, and show the life cycle of a system.

Functional

Identify what business logic is required for a given capability, list requirements applicable to a given capability, and show what business processes help realize a given capability

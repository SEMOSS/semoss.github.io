Open Analytics Environment

Seamoss is an innovative and data driven application that allows users to explore and uncover connections among existing data from multiple repositories.

Download Github

Setup Instructions

Step 1.

Download and extract the .zip file to the folder of your choosing.

Step 2.

To launch the tool click on the “RunGraphDB.bat” file located within the graph tool package you saved to your computer. A command prompt will appear followed by the application. Note: It may take a few minutes to open the tool, depending on your computer.

Benefits

Consolidate

Consolidate all of your information into a central location

Identify

Identify ripple effects associated with interface changes

Plan

Assist with planning and migration efforts. Support development of plans and estimates for modernizing systems to a net-centric enviroment based on Service Oriented Architecture (SOA) principles

Explore

Explore data and uncover previously unknown relationships

Who it's for

Leadership

  • Provide a “Big Picture” view of the Enterprise
  • Illustrate the benefits of net-centric environment based on SOA principles
  • Cross-reference data on Centrally and Service-managed systems
  • Create system lists by location/MTF

Technical

  • Highlight system and interface dependencies
  • Illustrate which interfaces can be replaced by services
  • Show what systems are providing/consuming a given service
  • List a system’s HW and SW components.

Financial

  • Provide a system’s total budget
  • List transition items associated with an ICD
  • Identify transition costs
  • Show the life cycle of a system
  • List budget items for a given system (by FY)

Functional

  • Identify what business logic is required for a given capability
  • List requirements applicable to a given capability
  • Show what business processes help realize a given capability

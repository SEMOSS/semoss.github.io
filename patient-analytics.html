Patient Analytics




The Affordable Care Act and Federal Medicare guidelines incentivize providers to optimize patient outcomes to reduce costs and the need for repeat visits.
Consequently, there is a growing marketplace need to provide analytics to identify key success factors that enable healthcare providers to beat payer expectations.

In order to tackle this problem, Semoss allows health care providers to probe and discover patterns around patients and environments by combining patient data as well as external secondary data sources (e.g. Epidemic Data, Environmental Data, etc) in aggregation to discover insights.

For example, Semoss can cross reference data from "Patients like Me" with past genetic information to uncover previously unknown connections or warn of possible future health issues for patients.

Semoss uniquely interprets meaningful and accurate information about a patient and the environment because it is able to consume data from multiple repositories, in multiple formats, with multiple dimensions. It is helpful to be able to explore data and traverse across repositories in a visual format thus providing the capability to predict future possibilities using analytics that are decoupled from the data. While most tools are able to tackle one or two of these issues, Semoss is a comprehensive solution.
